| **Budget** | $150,000 |

## Project Objectives
1. Conduct comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessment
2. Identify and prioritize security vulnerabilities (target: 90%+ of critical risks)
3. Develop actionable mitigation strategies
4. Establish incident response capabilities
5. Ensure PCI-DSS, GDPR, and ISO 27001 compliance
6. Deliver documentation and training materials

## Scope Summary

### In Scope:
- Infrastructure, application, network, and data security assessments
- Risk analysis and prioritization
- Mitigation strategy development
- Implementation planning
- Policy documentation
- Training material development

### Out of Scope:
- Physical implementation of security controls
- Complete infrastructure replacement
- Ongoing managed security services
- Mobile application development
- Marketing activities

## Key Stakeholders
- Executive Management
- IT Security Team
- IT Operations
- Compliance Officer
- External Auditors

## Success Criteria
- ✅ 90%+ of critical vulnerabilities identified
- ✅ Actionable mitigation strategies for all risks
- ✅ Stakeholder approval of recommendations
- ✅ Compliance gap closure plan accepted
- ✅ Project completed within budget and timeline
- ✅ Measurable security posture improvement
